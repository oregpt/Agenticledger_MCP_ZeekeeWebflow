### ğŸ’¡ ETHTaipei L2 Panel: A Gathering of Minds

The highlight of our ETHTaipei experience was the L2 panel featuring an ensemble of blockchain leaders, including Vitalik Buterin, Jordi Baylina, Yuan Su, Karl Floersch, and our very own Martin Derka. The discussion, buzzing over the recent updates on EIP-4844, revolved around strategies for making L2 solutions more affordable and secure.

### ğŸ’° Zircuit Bounties at ETHTaipei

Zircuit offered [three bounty categories](https://taikai.network/ethtaipei/hackathons/hackathon-2024/categories) at ETHTaipei, with prizes awarded in each category: best overall project, community and growth-focused project, and integration project. 19 submissions were received, showcasing smart contracts, beautiful front-ends, and forks of well-known codebases that were deployed on Zircuit. We're incredibly grateful for the help in testing our technology and fixing previous issues. The winners of our bounties are as follows:

-   Best Overall Project on Zircuit
    -   [Rift](https://taikai.network/ethtaipei/hackathons/hackathon-2024/projects/cltwdsfxr0d32w201641xisb8): The world's first instantaneous, gasless, cross-chain bridge
    -   [CrossYield](https://taikai.network/ethtaipei/hackathons/hackathon-2024/projects/cltreqlo80a5ewc01nl7op85e): A platform that collateralizes assets cross-chain for self-repaying loans utilizing Circle's CCTP and account abstraction to provide a seamless user experience
-   Community and Growth
    -   [Gridlock](https://taikai.network/ethtaipei/hackathons/hackathon-2024/projects/clu4osjtr0kf6y501quu3jxwf): Gridlock introduces spatial NFTs that are situated in a grid. On top of this grid, we implement a Catan/Risk type game where players can harvest resources and raise armies to attack their neighbors
    -   [Shishimaru](https://taikai.network/ethtaipei/hackathons/hackathon-2024/projects/clu40l9l40irkwc01f2v36xfk): Aimed at providing pet owners with a secure, transparent, and intermediary-free platform for pet walking services
-   Integrations
    -   [UniswapV3 for Zircuit](https://taikai.network/ethtaipei/hackathons/hackathon-2024/projects/clu4ez8xs0jsey501ydezfmja): Deployment of Uniswap v3 to the Zircuit testnet, and integration of the Uniswap interface to support the Zircuit testnet
    -   [Kage](https://taikai.network/ethtaipei/hackathons/hackathon-2024/projects/clu3z5q8a0iq4wc01x67g2mh3): Cross-chain private payment powered by stealth address and ERC-4337
