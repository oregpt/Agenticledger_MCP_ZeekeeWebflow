# Zircuit @ ETHGlobal NYC 2025

**Source:** https://www.zircuit.com/blog/zircuit-ethglobal-nyc-2025

---

Zircuit was proud to participate in the ETHGlobal New York City 2025 Hackathon. The event ran from August 15th to 17th in the heart of Manhattan. For this event, Zircuit decided to give out five prizes in two categories: best project on Zircuit (two winners) and best killer app idea (three winners). The first category is the classic catch-all for projects that impressed us, and the second was to submit no-code ideas. We tried it in Cannes over the summer and were impressed with the potential dApp applications so much that we decided to try it again. New York didn‚Äôt disappoint. We received nearly 30 prize submissions and had to make some very difficult decisions about who won. Below, we highlight our winners and look forward to making even more difficult decisions about winners in Buenos Aires in November!

### **First Place: Zutchi**

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/652dc770169423e16ac4a7da/68a7406ce86e2c32bbf5d033_AD_4nXcFcLDyN6dnILYPtw9BkcWne1QX2HsQkQPpNYO5WSr-_kOKpHo2vbVBN-qGQxGqLGyXmLtCQdI9wCG_dySKjX5R2B5fTpVrGfp8zoYleXQ_8LN-aklLd8iAtw-BUbyZhZ1nhr4W8w.png)

[Zutchi](https://ethglobal.com/showcase/zutchi-fnsx1) brought lovable, adorable, and maybe even profitable, pets on-chain. The gamified idea behind Zutchi was to adopt an on-chain cat that used the most adorable on-brand assets for Zircuit and keep them happy and healthy. Users were required to feed their cats, ensure they had enough rest, and even put them to ‚Äúwork‚Äù. That last part was interesting: the work was potentially depositing funds onto DeFi projects on Zircuit. A cute synergy described was the ability to deposit funds into the pet ‚Äì satisfying the feeding action ‚Äì and putting those funds to work to generate yield. The idea of an on-chain pet is fun, but the ability to build in real yield (while benefiting from Zircuit‚Äôs [Sequencer Level Security](https://docs.zircuit.com/architecture-and-concepts/sequencer-level-security-sls)) seems like a nice way to onboard inexperienced users. Check out the project to see the idea in action!

### **Second Place: Real-World Access**

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/652dc770169423e16ac4a7da/68a7406ce86e2c32bbf5d024_AD_4nXfPhvHlBYkE_cUAVD7JMzR-gi4wraFAP1ynCuoKqjixIQjOBjy8h4Yo06Fp3esosWk0HYpHNWnolaUeIHEPMScOCQiy0v5uL9lcVhh4Ltzcd34jloIs6aXYlBvsJC-NhlEkVASuVg.jpeg)

[Real-World Access](https://ethglobal.com/showcase/real-world-access-zoyaw) showcased a simple yet effective use of blockchain technology: smart contracts that unlock doors and locks in the real world. They were able to generate QR codes that could be scanned at the physical lock to unlock it, provided that smart contracts had received the appropriate funds and the owner validated the appropriate requirements. Some additional work, like an integration with a reputation system with the anonymization of assets or identities could enable fully automated cheap rentals without payment issues. And it wouldn't hurt to rename it to avoid confusion with _Real World Assets_. Nonetheless, the execution was solid and we look forward to the team‚Äôs next steps!

### **OathLock**

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/652dc770169423e16ac4a7da/68a7406ce86e2c32bbf5d02a_AD_4nXcOY4I2TG8EBwvp7cEVWm7E5Pc4WtNxYt5EC6xMZ4NNSaU0eLIbr-u7DS6nyQ29dPNOOw2htDJzgE3Y8KX7xd1JOm0M6EXxBrsiZfQWEzSNsI1PYyhS5ZlUa9duUmIQaFu_Tr701A.png)

[OathLock](https://ethglobal.com/showcase/oathlock-0e14g) aims to bring the massive success of Shopify on-chain. There‚Äôs nothing revolutionary about that, but it‚Äôs the kind of infrastructure that, if done well, can bring behind-the-scenes blockchain adoption to the masses. ### **VibeFlow**

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/652dc770169423e16ac4a7da/68a7406ce86e2c32bbf5d036_AD_4nXeb9VLvWuIFAdC84inxng3JAP8Sj2hefmy2mAjT2ddMwFORlF838tu9ycZb4MdQPYsog0g4fghYmAtbwvUtlsqHFuk7RZjzsX-zy4smX_vvcPnonUToBsvwGIsCuFlTpllaC9-P.png)

[VibeFlow](https://ethglobal.com/showcase/vibeflow-8hib4) aims to match people with similar interests for platonic friendships. ### **RelAI**

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/652dc770169423e16ac4a7da/68a7406ce86e2c32bbf5d030_AD_4nXf94dspuIlVbL1uAQobohl8dteSkK7ReH4iMGd6PyMmeedeDLjA5Aosktr6U9GRkFD61oAu99_-HXuBlQLotfOhBUjYwFZb-TxwD7sFf9QkgJssgSe0HP6_0vRIpkwhTiQGKwDCOQ.png)

[RelAI](https://ethglobal.com/showcase/relai-bozvt) aimed to build out a decentralized reputation registry for AI agents.
