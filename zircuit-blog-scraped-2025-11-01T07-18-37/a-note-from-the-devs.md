We also sent ETH back** to every account that bridged to Zircuit the first day, and took the opportunity to put some pressure on the network following the reset. We resolved the other two issues later in the week without any additional state impact.¬†

In all cases, and after a short period of reduced availability, the network continued in normal operations. **We are appreciative of the smooth coordination with our partners at** [**Chainstack**](https://chainstack.com/) who operate the public node that all of you connect to!¬†
